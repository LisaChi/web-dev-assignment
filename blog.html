In The Beginning

The beginning of the process, I have nothing but a blank page with "hello world" written on it. From those two words, I am going able to make a website that is unique and represents me (sometimes blunt and straightforward as well as colourful). As with every start of a large project, the task before me seems daunting but with every line of code added the finish line nears me.

-Lisa

The Progression

At the beginning of this assignment, I was finding it easy to create a basic layout, but the colour schemes have been difficult. I think I like the aqua, white and grey theme that I have going on because it is the right amount of colour whilst being different. I found myself thinking that plain backgrounds were too generic and that I should create something a bit more eye-catching. The background was hard to create but I am happy with the final result, and I think I want to move on and create my portfolio page which has images that have the title hover over the top (sounds complex but I am ready to handle this new challenge). I am super excited to see where this vision of mine takes me.

-Lisa

The End

I am nearing the end of my journey with this website. It is not the absolute end but where I should save this checkpoint by handing in my assignment. Adding the hover effect to my portfolio was a mission and a half but I am super happy with how it turned out and I am liking the look of my site report. I am slowly ticking all the boxes that had taunted me at the beginning of the project and I am super confident in the work I have produced. This experience has allowed me to express myself and discover new changes within my own personality. Goodbye for now.

-Lisa
