My conclusion

Due to my background being in section tags the HTML validator wants me to change my background to either have text or use a div. Since I am not allowed to use divs in my code and I do not want a word in my background, I can not fix these warnings. This has given all of my pages the same warning text which is why this message is repeated multiple times. Furthermore, another warning I have received is misusing the h1 tag. All of the times I use the h1 tag I intend it on being a title to a section of a page which is why there are multiple. They are there to symbolise a heading that separates the columns even further. They are not subheadings.
